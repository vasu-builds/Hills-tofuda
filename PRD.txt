01. Brand Soul &amp; Design Philosophy
Hills Tofuda is not just a tofu brand. It is Nainital in a block — misty mountains, clean air, honest food. Every pixel of this website must feel like that. No stock photo energy. No generic food-brand templates. This is a place-based, character-led, story-first brand.

The one thing someone must remember
Tofuda Da baat karta hai.
Not a logo. Not a product. A character who lives on this site — who guides, who jokes, who sells. Everything else is set design. Tofuda Da is the soul.

Aesthetic direction — Organic Luxury Editorial
Think: a Nainital cafe&apos;s handwritten menu meets a luxury food magazine. Warm, earthy, confident. Not minimalist to the point of coldness. Not maximalist to the point of noise. The aesthetic sits in the specific zone of &quot;this was designed by someone who actually loves this food and this place.&quot;

TONE WORDS
Warm — like a Kumaoni kitchen
Confident — not apologetic about tofu
Local — Nainital pride, not generic India
Playful — Tofuda Da ki awaaz
Premium — without being unrelatable
WHAT THIS IS NOT
Not purple-gradient AI slop
Not generic food delivery app
Not corporate health-food brand
Not trying to look like a startup
Not boring serif on white

03. Animation &amp; Motion System
Philosophy
Every animation earns its place. Motion is storytelling — it guides the eye, reveals content, creates emotion. No animations just for showing off. The site should feel alive, not restless.

Easing Curves — The Secret to Premium Feel
Name
Curve + When to use
Snappy
cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1) — Primary. Feels premium. Use for reveals, entries.
Gentle
cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1) — Hover states, micro-interactions.
Spring
Framer Motion spring: stiffness 300, damping 30 — Tofuda Da bounce, playful moments.
Linear
Linear — Only for scroll progress bars, never for reveals.

Scroll Animation Rules
Trigger: element enters viewport at 80% (not 100% — too late)
Stagger delay between siblings: 0.08s
Default reveal: opacity 0→1, y: 40px→0, duration 0.7s
Never animate more than 4 elements simultaneously
Images: scale 1.05→1 as they enter (subtle Ken Burns feel)
Mobile: reduce y offset to 20px, reduce duration to 0.5s

Libraries
Framer Motion — Primary. useScroll, useTransform, AnimatePresence, motion.div
GSAP + ScrollTrigger — Complex timelines (How to order section, Tofuda Da parallax)
Lenis — Smooth scroll inertia (lerp: 0.08). Synced with GSAP ticker.
Spline — 3D tofu block in hero. Embed via @splinetool/react-spline.


04. Spline 3D Integration
What Spline does here
Hero section mein ek 3D tofu block float karega — rotate karta hua, soft shadows ke saath. Yeh woh &quot;wow&quot; moment hai jo site ko immediately alag karta hai. Koi generic product photo nahi — ek living, breathing 3D object.

Setup
Create 3D tofu model in Spline (spline.design) — free account se shuru karo
Export as Spline scene URL
npm install @splinetool/react-spline @splinetool/runtime
Lazy load it — only loads when hero is visible
Fallback: static image if Spline fails to load (mobile ki liye)
Important:
Spline 3D heavy hota hai. Sirf hero mein use karo. Baaki site pe CSS animations karo. Mobile pe Spline disable karo — image fallback show karo instead.

Hero Composition
Left 50%: Headline + tagline + CTAs (text animates in staggered)
Right 50%: Spline 3D tofu block (auto-rotates, mouse-reactive)
Background: Illustrated SVG Himalayan mountains (parallax on scroll)
Bottom right: Tofuda Da illustration (GSAP float animation)
Overlay: Subtle grain texture at 3% opacity for warmth

Mobile Responsiveness Rules
Mobile-first CSS — write mobile styles first, desktop as override
Breakpoints: sm 640px, md 768px, lg 1024px, xl 1280px
Spline 3D: disabled on mobile (image fallback)
GSAP animations: simplified on mobile (less y offset, shorter duration)
Touch-friendly: all tap targets min 44x44px
WhatsApp button: always above fold on mobile
Lenis: disabled on mobile (native scroll is smoother on touch)
Images: WebP format, Next/image for automatic optimization
Font sizes: fluid with clamp() — no fixed px on hero text
